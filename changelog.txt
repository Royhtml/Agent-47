
## Game Information & Description

**Game Title:** AGENT 47

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Short Description:**

AGENT 47 is an exploration and narrative-driven puzzle-adventure game set against the backdrop of Indonesian culture. Players step into the shoes of an elite hacker on a mission to expose and eradicate corrupt officials. Navigate through secret networks and local islands, solve intricate cyber-puzzles, interact with local informants, and gather classified evidence to uncover an ancient conspiracy deeply rooted in modern greed.

## Key Features

* **2.5D Art Style Open World:** Explore diverse Indonesian environments, from dense jungles and coastal areas to local villages and high-tech corrupt corporate facilities.
* **Environment & Cyber-Based Puzzles:** Solve puzzles by manipulating physical environments, hacking security systems, and bypassing digital firewalls.
* **Dynamic NPCs & Local Intel:** Interact with local citizens and informants to unlock side quests, gather intel, and uncover regional lore.
* **Hacking & Inventory System:** Manage a streamlined inventory, upgrade your hacking tools, and decrypt stolen files.
* **Traditional-Themed Soundtrack:** Immersive music and sound effects blending traditional Indonesian instruments with modern cyberpunk synths.

---

## How to Play

1. Use the **Arrow keys** or **WASD** to move.
2. Press **E** to interact with objects, NPCs, and terminals.
3. Open the **Map/Database** to track objectives, target profiles, and collected evidence.
4. Solve puzzles by combining found items, decrypting codes, or manipulating the environment.
